Elements in Chem Inc. have multitude of purposes. The first of which is for the production of funds. Each element is displayed along with three values. In the upper left it the current amount owned (10). The next, in the top right, is the total production of that element per second (50). Finally, at the botton is the current purchase price (100).

Outisde of purchasing elements, reseach is the main method of progression in Chem Inc. Scientists can be purchased with funds, which will produce research points over time. These research points can be used to purchase three separate methods of progression:

New Elements
Fairly self explainatory, new elements can be purchased with research points. After purchase, these elements are unlocked in the periodic table for purchase with funds.

New Upgrade Packs
Upgrades are the primary way to increase element production in Chem Inc. Upgrade packs must be purchased with research points to unlock these upgrades, which are found in the aptly named "Upgrades" pannel.
